FAQ — Coated SBS 350 GSM

Coated SBS 350 GSM is ideal for premium packaging — cosmetics, luxury retail boxes, pharma cartons, and high-quality point-of-sale displays where print finish and stiffness matter.

Yes — SBS provides an excellent printing surface with sharp colours and consistent ink hold. It’s compatible with CMYK, spot colours, and premium finishing like UV and foil.

Absolutely — foil stamping, embossing/debossing, soft-touch lamination, and spot UV are all supported on 350 GSM SBS. We recommend prototyping to confirm desired effects.

SBS is virgin-fiber board but it is recyclable and can be used with responsible sourcing. For higher recycled content, consider recycled duplex alternatives — we can advise on trade-offs between print finish and recycled content.

Coated SBS provides premium whiteness, smoothness, stiffness, and the best print quality compared to Duplex or Art Board — ideal for luxury and FMCG packaging.

Yes. Its structural strength supports premium cartons, rigid-style sleeves, and printed boxes. Add lamination or UV for increased durability.
